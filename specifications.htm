Of particular interest to you may be our latest certification to Boeing BAC 5851 Class 2 coatings. These HVOF coatings have provided, in many cases, a viable approved alternative to the single source Union Carbide (Praxair) Detonation Gun Process.

FSI has a complete onsite metallurgical lab for the evaluation and testing of Thermal Sprayed coatings. The basic equipment includes tensile testers, metallographic scope, DPH and Rockwell Hardness testers, mounting press and sample polishing/grinding and cutting equipment.

Our Quality Control Manual complies with MIL I 45208A and our calibration system is in compliance with MIL STD 45662. By the first quarter of 2001 we will be ready to apply for our ISO 9000 Certificate.
