Training & Technical Services

 

 

 

Another key to FSI's success is our unique "Operator Training Program." This program was developed in 1972 and has been ongoing ever since. The Thermal Spray process is extremely operator technique dependent and as we learned early on, it is almost impossible to hire a trained qualified Thermal Spray operator. The training program FSI developed has a comprehensive curriculum that insures potential operators have a thorough understanding of the theory and hands-on approach to all aspects of the Thermal Spray process. This program takes approximately 6 months to complete and upon completion operators receive a certificate defining their achievements in various skill levels. The training program is then restarted and is perpetual which provides all of our personnel a constant reinforcement of the requirements necessary to achieve a successful coating. Our instructors are currently teaching thermal spray coating methods to military and civilian personnel around the world.  The instructors can teach your personnel the requirements of dozens of specifications such as:

 

DOD-STD-2138

MIL. STD. 1687 (SH)

MIL. STD. 896 USAF

Pratt & Whitney PWA53

Boeing BAC 5851
