Why use Flame Spraying?

Less expensive

Repairs generally cost less than replacing with new parts.

Wear Resistance

Flame sprayed coatings often outwear new parts two to three times.

Safer

Flame Spraying does not produce distortion or affect temper of heat treated parts that other repair methods cause.

Saves Time

Turn-around time can be as low as a few hours.

Advances

New process innovations allow us to apply exotic wear resistant materials onto less wear resistant parts.
